Arduino Board Manager:
- ESP32 by Espressif Systems (v3.x or later)

Arduino Libraries (install via Library Manager):
- LiquidCrystal_I2C (by Frank de Brabander)
- ArduinoJson (by Benoit Blanchon, v7.x or later)

Hardware Requirements:
- ESP32-S3 Board (with PSRAM recommended)
- OV3660 Camera Module
- LCD1602 with I2C module (Address 0x27 or 0x3F)
- 5V Relay Module
- Active Buzzer
